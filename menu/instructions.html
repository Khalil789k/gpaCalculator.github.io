Instructions

Welcome to our GPA Calculator! Follow these simple steps to calculate your GPA:

  1. Enter your course name in the "Course Name" field.
  2. Enter the number of credit hours for the course in the "Credit Hours" field.
  3. Enter your marks for the course in the "Marks" field (out of 100).
  4. Click the "Add Course" button to add more courses if needed.
  5. Click the "Calculate" button to calculate your GPA.

Your GPA will be displayed at the bottom of the page once you click "Calculate." If you have any questions or need assistance, please don't hesitate to contact us.
