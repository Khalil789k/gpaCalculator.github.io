GPA Calculator Instructions

Welcome to the GPA Calculator. Follow these steps to calculate your GPA accurately:

1. Course Information:

In the "Course Name" field, enter the name of your course.

In the "Credit Hours" field, input the number of credit hours associated with the course. Credit hours represent the amount of work required for the course.

In the "Marks" field, enter your achieved marks for the course. Marks should be out of 100.

2. Adding Courses:

Click the button if you have more courses to add. This allows you to include multiple courses in your GPA calculation. Repeat the process for each course you want to include.

3. Calculate Your GPA:

Once you've entered all your course information, click the button to compute your GPA.
